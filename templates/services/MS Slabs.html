MS Slabs

MS Slabs (like MS slab ingots) can either be sold directly to other businesses/factories for further rolling or can be rolled by us to produce MS plates and coils, depending on market scenario, demand, and price at that time.

The Basic features of MS Slabs are:

Malleable

Can be beaten into a thin sheet easily without breaking or cracking. Thus, make the steel workable and mouldable.

Ductile

This feature make flexible as it can be beaten into a thin wire. It makes the end product more strong.

Semi-Finished, Rectangular and Flat

Wide rectangular shaped hot-rolls from an ingot or strand cast. They can be used for manufacturing of any flat steel product.

Corrosive

As it is not stainless, thus it is corrosive.
